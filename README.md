##  Key Insights

###  Sales Trends

* Median sales increased by **over 9% within two months**, indicating short-term growth momentum.

###  Loyalty Points Analysis

* The **UK** generated the highest total loyalty points among all countries.

###  Revenue by Category

* **Gardening products** contributed the largest share of total revenue.

###  Profit Margins

* Significant margin improvement observed in **early October 2023**.

###  Anomaly Detection

* Revenue spikes identified in **late August and early September**, signaling potential promotional or seasonal effects.
